## About

I am Fang Qingyun (方青云), Ph.D., based at Tsinghua University in Beijing, China. My research focuses on remote sensing object detection, multispectral and cross-modality information fusion, lightweight perception models, and onboard intelligent processing for imaging satellites.

My recent work studies how to make visual detection systems more reliable and deployable under remote sensing conditions, especially when combining heterogeneous sensing modalities or running perception models with limited onboard resources.

## Research Interests

- **Remote sensing object detection:** robust detection methods for complex aerial and satellite imagery.
- **Multispectral and cross-modality fusion:** feature-level and decision-level fusion for visible, infrared, and multispectral sensing.
- **Lightweight onboard intelligence:** model compression, feature distillation, and efficient perception for constrained platforms.
- **Evidence fusion and evaluation:** detection fusion, uncertainty handling, and evidence-theoretic reasoning for multi-source observations.
